Full Script:
Inspect the eastern sentry-post.
Rosie: What is the goal or first step of inspect?
Go to the eastern sentry-post.
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If the lightswitch is off, then turn off the lightswitch.
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If you are in a sentry-post and an extinguisher is not present, then fetch an extinguisher from the supply-room. 
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If the current location is empty, then turn off the lightswitch.
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
You are done. 

Raise a fire-alarm.
Rosie: What is the goal or first step of raise?
Remember the current location as the emergency location.
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
Go to the eastern hallway.
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
Turn on the fire alarm.
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
Say "There is a fire." to the CO
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
The CO is in the CO's office.
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
Say the emergency location to the CO. 
Rosie: What is the next step of raise?
You are done. 
Whenever you see a fire, then raise a fire-alarm.

Guard the barracks.
Rosie: What is the goal or first step of guard?
Ask "Who is my relieving officer?"
Rosie: Who is my relieving officer?
Williams.
Rosie: What is the next step of guard?
Remember the answer as the relieving officer.
Rosie: What is the next step of guard?
Repeat the following actions until the relieving officer is present.

Inspect the mess-hall.
(Rosie goes to the mess-hall)
Interrupt inspect. 
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If you are in the mess-hall and a plate is on the table, then move the plate into the sink.
What is the goal or first action of move?
The goal is that the plate is in the sink. 
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If you are in the mess-hall and a condiment is on the table, then store the condiment. 
What is the goal or first action of store?
If the object is a condiment, then the goal is that the condiment is in the fridge and the fridge is closed. 
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
Continue inspect
Rosie: What is the next step of guard?
Inspect the eastern sentry post.
Rosie: What is the next step of guard?

Inspect the motor-pool. 
Interrupt inspect. 
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
If you are in the motor-pool, do the following tasks.
Ensure a sentry is present. 
Rosie: Is there another task?
Ensure a dispatcher is present. 
Rosie: Is there another task?
If a vehicle is unlocked, lock it. 
Rosie: Is there another task?
You are done
Rosie: What is the next step of inspect?
Continue inspect
Rosie: What is the next step of guard?
Repeat.
